Programming experience

    About four years ago I thought of exploring different areas of programming. I decided to develop my first video game that my friends could download and enjoy playing. The game is called “Fun Run” and the concept is to make the character jump over obstacles. Later, In September 2020 I completed my first major programming project called “WordQuiz”. The main objective of this website is to help users learn new words by making a list of them and then having to answer. Seeking to further develop my IT skills, I took the “HTML, CSS, and Javascript for Web Developers” online course by Johns Hopkins University and earned a certificate.
