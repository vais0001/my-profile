How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

     First of all, you need to go to this link and log in with your MyHZ account. Then you will be able to upload all of the necessary documents that you want to print. Next, you have to go to the printer in the HZ building, scan your student card and the printing will start.

How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?

     All information about scanning documents can be found on this HZ page .

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event at Friday 16/09) on the HZ web shop?

     You enter the Webshop page and then you need to log in. There you will be able to find and purchase tickets or subscribtions.

Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID?

     Every little bit of invormation regarding COVID can be found on this HZ page.

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

     You can book a project space by going to this HZ page. Then go to Make a reservation -> Reserve meeting room or Project Group room -> Reserve a room and choose a time slot that fits you.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

     You must show your HZ pass when going through the barriers.
